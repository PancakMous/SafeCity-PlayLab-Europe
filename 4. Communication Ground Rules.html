Ground Rules

OUR VOICE IS
OUR IMPACT.

Consistency is key. Whether we are writing a report for the EU Commission or talking to a 14-year-old student, we speak with SafeCity's unique DNA.

Verbal Energy

The SafeCity Approach

Institutional Jargon (Avoid)

"We are facilitating a multi-stakeholder dialogue regarding urban safety metrics."

The SafeCity Voice (Prefer)

"We turn youth ideas into real data for safer cities. We're building the future together."

Empower

Call students "active contributors," never "victims."

Specify

Mention "women and girls" specifically, not just "citizens."

Written Impact

The Active Voice Rule

"SafeCity empowers youth" vs. "Youth are empowered by SafeCity"

Structure for Speed

Use bold headers and short paragraphs. Our reports should be as accessible as our game.

Institutional Pride

Always acknowledge Erasmus+ funding. It's our badge of quality and transnational cooperation.

NEVER DO THIS:

"Never dismiss safety concerns as 'subjective' or 'emotional.' In the SafeCity world, perceived safety is the most valuable data we have."

ONE VISION.
ONE VOICE.

By following these rules, we ensure that SafeCity PlayLab Europe remains a scalable, documented, and innovative social intervention model across the EU.
